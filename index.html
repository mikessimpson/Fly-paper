About

My fascination with airplanes, airports, and airlines began as a small child. Both of my parents were teachers. As a result, my family could afford the annual summertime trip by plane to see

the grandparents in Texas. It was a result of those trips that I caught "the bug". Fast forward to my adulthood: I've always considered myself lucky to have been employed in the industry that

I absolutely loved. The old saying is if you love what you do, it isn't work. As such, I spent nearly 30 years in the airline industry, based primaily based at Los Angeles International Airport

My professional roles included stints in administration, operations, and sales. I also enjoyed brief stints at John Wayne Orange County Airport, as well as Ontario International Airport.

As I'm now enjoying semi-retirement, I'm creating my next act - taking my passion for aviation and conbining it with my passion for reading. My next act is Fly-paper Books & Magazines....an online bookstore that specializes in

....yes, aviation. You will find new & used books, and back issue magazines. My thematic approach is primarily the airline industry.

Michael Simpson / Fly-paper Books & Magazines
